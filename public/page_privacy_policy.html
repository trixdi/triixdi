Privacy Policy for Trixdi

This Privacy Policy outlines how Trixdi handles and protects your data.

Image Data Storage

We may store image data for users who choose to save their data within the app.

Data Deletion

Users have the right to delete all their data stored in Trixdi at any time. This can be done by sending an email.

Analytics and User Attribution

Trixdi does not collect analytics that can be attributed to individual users. Our focus is on improving the app's functionality while respecting user privacy.

Third-Party Data Sharing

Trixdi does not share your data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary for the functionality of the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the app or via email, as appropriate.

User Consent

By using Trixdi, users consent to the collection and use of their data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
